This roundtable discussion presents findings from a comparative analysis of how mathematical application problems are framed and presented in US and Chinese secondary textbooks. The research examines text characteristics, problem-solving contexts, and implications for mathematics literacy instruction across different educational systems.

**Key Discussion Points:**
- Text complexity and linguistic features in mathematical application problems
- Cultural contexts and real-world applications in mathematics education
- Implications for preparing teachers to support mathematics literacy development
