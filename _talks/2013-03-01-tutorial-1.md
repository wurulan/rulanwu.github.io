This presentation reports findings from a systematic review examining research on heritage language development in immigrant children. The synthesis integrates evidence from multiple studies to identify key factors supporting heritage language maintenance and identity development.

**Presentation Topics:**
- Heritage language exposure and maintenance in immigrant families
- Relationships between heritage language use and identity development
- Implications for family literacy practices and community language programs
- Recommendations for supporting multilingual youth and families
