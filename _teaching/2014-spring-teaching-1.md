**Course Description:**
This course provides Chinese language and cultural instruction to K-12 students with varying levels of prior Chinese exposure. The curriculum emphasizes communicative competence, cultural understanding, and heritage language development.

**Course Objectives:**
- Develop listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills in Mandarin Chinese
- Foster appreciation and understanding of Chinese culture and traditions
- Support heritage language maintenance for students with Chinese family backgrounds
- Build confidence and motivation for continued language learning

**Teaching Methods:**
- Communicative language teaching approaches
- Interactive and culturally-responsive activities
- Integration of authentic materials and cultural resources
- Differentiated instruction for mixed-ability learners
