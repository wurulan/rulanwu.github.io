**Role Description:**
As a Graduate Research Assistant, I support faculty research projects while developing my expertise in literacy education, teacher preparation, and cross-linguistic research.

**Responsibilities:**
- Conduct literature reviews and synthesize research findings
- Support data collection and qualitative analysis for research projects
- Participate in collaborative research including:
  - Aggie Teachers Trajectory: Teacher perspectives on literacy education
  - Asian Americans' Motivation and Career Exploration: Community language programs
  - Math Literacy in Teacher Education: Comparative textbook analysis
- Present research findings at academic conferences

**Skills Developed:**
- Advanced research methods and data analysis
- Academic writing and publication preparation
- Qualitative coding and synthesis
- Collaborative research practices
