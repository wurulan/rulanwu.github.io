This research examines and compares the text characteristics of mathematical application problems in textbooks used in the United States and China. The study investigates how mathematical concepts are contextualized and presented to students across different educational systems.

**Key Focus Areas:**
- Text complexity and linguistic features
- Problem-solving contexts and real-world applications
- Pedagogical implications for mathematics literacy instruction
